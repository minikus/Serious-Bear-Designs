Serious Bear Design was co-founded by freelance designers Daniel Handaru and Carmen Minikus in Sydney Australia, combining their expertise within the digital, print and industrial design fields in order to give clients a comprehensive creative package from start to finish. From logo and branding design through to responsive web services, promotional materials and email marketing, our creative agency can construct every element of a business' online and offline identity.

We are a collaborative and business-minded team driven by a passion for creating effective and remarkable design. We are warm and friendly serious bears, but we don't bite.
